Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe to make some randomass lasagna I found on the internet. I hope it tastes good lol.

Ingredients

  • Big Flat Pasta
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Sausage

Steps

  1. Lay one layer of pasta in pan
  2. layer sausage and tomatoe sauce on sheet of pasta
  3. Lay another layer of pasta and repeat
  4. Repeat until pan is filled
